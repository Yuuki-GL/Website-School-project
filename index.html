About Mytimeline

Mytimeline is your personal event organizer, designed to help you keep track of all your important moments and milestones. With a clean, user-friendly interface, you can easily add, view, and manage your events in a single, chronological timeline.

This website uses your browser's local storage to save your timeline data, ensuring your events are always available even when you close the tab.
